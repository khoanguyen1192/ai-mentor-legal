# Privacy Policy

**AI Mentor: Mind Sanctuary**
Last updated: April 28, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Account data**: Email address and display name when you register.
- **Journal entries**: Text you write in the journaling feature, stored in your private account.
- **Mood logs**: Mood ratings and optional notes from daily check-ins.
- **Conversation history**: Messages between you and the AI Mentor.
- **Device data**: Device type and OS version for crash reporting only.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and personalize the AI coaching experience.
- To display your mood trends and wellness analytics.
- To send optional daily reminder notifications (only if you enable them).
- We **never** sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third parties.

## 3. Data Storage & Security
Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore), protected by Firebase Authentication. All data is tied to your private account and inaccessible to other users.

## 4. AI Processing
Messages sent to the AI Mentor are processed by third-party AI providers (Google Gemini / OpenRouter) solely to generate responses. These providers do not store your data for training purposes under our agreements.

## 5. Data Retention & Deletion
You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings screen. Upon deletion, all journal entries, mood logs, and conversation history are permanently removed within 30 days.

## 6. Children's Privacy
AI Mentor is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
